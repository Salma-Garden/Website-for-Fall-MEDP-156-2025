Hello! I am Salma and I don't describe myself as an artist but someone who simply loves art in many forms. From the art of storytelling in the words of a novel to all the work that goes into a video game. Most of what I make comes from curiosity and the desire to experiment with different materials, so I am self-teaching myself how to draw at the moment with different books. I practice both digital art and traditional with pen and paper, I specifically use pen so I don't try to be perfectionest, spending more time correcting tha actually drawing.
